The Story

I didn't start in marketing. I started in design — learning to see before I learned to strategize. Four years at the intersection of visual craft and research at Gartner taught me that the most powerful brands aren't built on big budgets. They're built on clarity: a sharp point of view, communicated without waste.

"The best brand work doesn't feel like marketing. It feels like recognition."

An MBA from IIM Kozhikode sharpened the business side of that instinct — adding fluency in GTM strategy, customer segmentation, and growth frameworks to what was already a design-first lens. Today, at CAMS, I lead marketing across a portfolio of products that touch millions of investors and thousands of distributors in India's mutual fund ecosystem.

I'm drawn to the complex ones — the categories that don't market themselves, where trust has to be built word by word and campaign by campaign. Fintech, compliance, alternatives. The spaces where good storytelling is the competitive advantage nobody talks about.
